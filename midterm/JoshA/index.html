JOSH A

I've been a fan of Josh A, an Orlando-based rapper/producer who's been making music since 2015. He's labeled as an emo rap artist, however he has ventured outside of that realm into more generic rap, screamo and even pop. He co-produced most of his discography with his friend Jake Hill, who is a common feature and collaborator on Josh A's work. He draws on inspiration from artists such as NF, Logic and Lil Peep. His career first blew up from producing his own music and also making meme music for the Call of Duty: Zombies community.
