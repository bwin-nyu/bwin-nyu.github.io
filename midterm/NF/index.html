I discovered NF in sophomore year of high school. He's a Michigan-based rapper who focuses on lyrical flow, thundering opera samples and heavy bass. He draws heavily on artists such as Eminem, and he has been compared to artists such as Logic and Machine Gun Kelly. Although he initially made his debut as a "Christian hip-hop artist", he has shifted his style/image far from it, even denying his label as a Christian rapper. His most notable record hit was "Let You Down" off of his album "Perception", althought my favorite track is "Like This" from the next album, "The Search".
