LOGIC

I've always known about Logic since his sophomore album, "The Incredible True Story", but I never really was that interested in him until my friends bought me a ticket to his tour for his mixtape "Bobby Tarantino II" where NF was opening for him. After listening to his music, I gave him a try by listening through almost all of his discography. However, I found his first album and last album to be his best work, where he uses more "traditional" rap/hip-hop beats that distinguish him from other artists. Logic's flow and energy is what draws me in the most, and his producer/friend 6ix always is able to produce music/beats that I really enjoy. I would say I've been a fan of Logic for around 3-4 years now. I chose "Metropolis" because this was the song that I listened to on the way back and forth from my first job in Brooklyn while riding the J/M train lines.
