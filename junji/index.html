Basic Overview

//slides images //slides content
  • Junji Ito is a Japanese horror manga artist.
  • Most notable works: Tomie, Uzumaki, Gyo.
  • Initially worked daytime job as dental technician.
//page 2 nonsense
  • Born in Nakatsugawa, Gifu, Japan on July 31, 1963.
  • First began reading horror magazines.
  • Many childhood experiences influenced works.
//page 3 nonsense
  • Lineart detailing style prominent in many other manga styles.
  • Great attention to detail with gore.
  • Emotions of characters reflected in their eyes.
    • Horror.
    • Disgust.
    • Malice.
  • Eerie, uncanny, and unsettling.
  • Transition from traditional method (ink and paper) to digital.
  • Influences: Kazuo Umezu, Hideshi Hino, Yasutaka Tsutsui, and H.P. Lovecraft
